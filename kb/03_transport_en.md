# Public Transit in Quebec

**Audience:** International students  
**Updated:** 2024

---

## 1. Transit Networks by City

### Lévis (UQAR Campus)
- **Réseau de transport de Lévis (RTL)**  
  Use the Chrono app for real-time schedules  
- **Quebec–Lévis Ferry**: boat crossing between Lévis and Quebec City (~10 min)  
  Student pricing available. Schedules at traversiers.com

### Quebec City (Université Laval)
- **Réseau de transport de la Capitale (RTC)**  
  Website: rtcquebec.ca  
  App: Chrono (real-time)  
  Monthly student pass: check current pricing on rtcquebec.ca

### Montreal (UQAM and others)
- **Société de transport de Montréal (STM)**  
  Website: stm.info  
  Metro (4 lines) + buses  
  Apps: Chrono or STM app  
  Monthly student pass (Opus card): check pricing on stm.info

---

## 2. Student Transit Card (Opus / Magnetic Card)

- The Opus card is used in Quebec City and Montreal (rechargeable)
- Student fares are significantly cheaper with valid enrollment proof
- Rechargeable online, at kiosks, or at metro stations

---

## 3. Useful Apps

| App | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Chrono** (free) | Real-time for RTC (Quebec City) and Exo (Montreal suburbs) |
| **STM app** | Real-time STM Montreal |
| **Google Maps** | Multi-modal route planning |
| **Netlift** | Carpooling between students in Quebec |
| **BlaBlaCar** | Long-distance carpooling |

---

## 4. Cycling and Soft Mobility

- **BIXI** (Montreal, Quebec City, Laval): bike-sharing service  
  Annual student membership available → bixi.com
- Quebec has an extensive cycling path network (best in summer)
- In winter, walking and public transit are preferred

---

## 5. Car Sharing

- **Communauto**: self-service cars (Montreal, Quebec City, Lévis)  
  Website: communauto.com  
  Affordable hourly rates, great option without a car

---

## 6. Practical Tips

- Download **Google Maps** or **Chrono** before arriving
- Note the bus lines between your housing and campus
- Plan alternatives for snowstorms or transit strikes
- In winter, dress warmly – some bus stops are outdoors
- Universities sometimes offer **free shuttle service** between campus and certain neighborhoods
