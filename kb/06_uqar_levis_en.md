# UQAR – Lévis Campus

**Université du Québec à Rimouski, Lévis Campus**  
**Updated:** 2024

---

## General Information

- **Address:** 1595, boul. Alphonse-Desjardins, Lévis (Québec) G6V 0A6
- **Official website:** uqar.ca
- **Campus profile:** The Lévis campus is smaller than the main Rimouski campus but offers several complete degree programs

---

## Programs Offered (Main)

- Business Administration (BAA) – including specializations
- Ethics
- Accounting Sciences
- Education Sciences
- Social Work
- Human Resources Management

⚠️ **Always verify the current complete list at uqar.ca** – programs change yearly.

---

## Registrar's Office (Registrariat)

- **Role:** Course registration, transcripts, graduation, program changes
- **Contact:** uqar.ca → Students → Registrariat
- **In person:** Student services counter at the Lévis campus

---

## International Student Services

- **Student Life and International Student Services Office**
- Assistance with immigration procedures, cultural adaptation, and housing
- Welcome programs at the start of each semester

---

## Student Portal

- **Mon portail UQAR**: portail.uqar.ca
- Register for courses, view grades, access transcripts
- Log in with your UQAR credentials (provided after admission)

---

## Campus Library

- Access to digital resources (Érudit, ProQuest, etc.)
- Study rooms available
- Hours: check uqar.ca (vary by semester)

---

## Institutional Email

- Automatically provided upon registration (format: firstname.lastname@uqar.ca)
- Use **only** this email to contact professors and university services

---

## Getting to Campus

- **RTL bus (Réseau de transport de Lévis)**: several lines serve the area – check the Chrono app
- **Quebec–Lévis Ferry**: if coming from Quebec City → fast and convenient (~10–15 min crossing)
- **Parking**: available on campus, annual or daily rates

---

## Student Life in Lévis

- **AÉUQAR**: UQAR's student association (Lévis branch)
- Sports activities: access to campus sports facilities
- **Cafeteria** available on campus
- **Housing**: UQAR Lévis has no large on-campus residences – housing is mainly in the city

**Best neighborhoods for housing:**
- Lévis city centre (Saint-Romuald, Saint-Nicolas, central Lévis)
- 10–30 min from campus by bus
