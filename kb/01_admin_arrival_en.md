# Administrative Steps on Arrival in Quebec

**Audience:** International students – new arrivals  
**Updated:** 2024

---

## 1. Social Insurance Number (SIN)

The SIN is required to work legally in Canada.

- **Who is eligible:** Study permit holders with work authorization
- **Where to get it:** Nearest Service Canada Centre
- **What to bring:** Original study permit, passport, proof of Canadian address
- **Processing time:** Usually issued the same day
- **Note:** SINs starting with "9" are for temporary residents – this is normal

---

## 2. Opening a Bank Account

Opening a bank account in the first weeks is essential for daily life.

**Student-friendly banks in Quebec:**
- **Desjardins** (credit union, widely present in Quebec)
- **TD Canada Trust** – Free Student Account
- **RBC (Royal Bank)** – Student Account
- **National Bank** – Newcomer Program

**Documents required:**
- Passport
- Study permit
- Canadian proof of address (or university acceptance letter)

---

## 3. Health Insurance

**Case 1 – Permanent Residents:**  
Register with the **Régie de l'assurance maladie du Québec (RAMQ)** upon arrival. A 3-month waiting period may apply.

**Case 2 – Study Permit Holders:**  
Generally **not covered by RAMQ**. Your university usually offers group insurance (e.g., ASEQ, Greenshield). Check with student services.

**In an emergency:** Go to a hospital or CLSC. You will be billed without insurance.

---

## 4. Finding Housing

**Common options:**
- **University residences** – book early, ideal for the first year
- **Kijiji.ca** – local classified ads
- **Facebook Marketplace / Local groups** – e.g., "Student Housing Quebec"
- **Roommates** – share an apartment with fellow students

**Vocabulary:**
- Studio / 1½ = room + integrated bathroom
- 3½ = living room + bedroom + kitchen + bathroom
- "Chauffé/éclairé" = heat and electricity included in rent

---

## 5. Phone Plan

**Affordable carriers for students:**
- **Public Mobile** – economical prepaid plans
- **Koodo Mobile** – flexible data and calls
- **Fido** – student offers available
- **Lucky Mobile** – cheaper, Rogers network
