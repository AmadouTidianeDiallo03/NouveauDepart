# Glossary of Quebec University Terms

**Audience:** International students  
**Updated:** 2024

---

## A

**Academic advisor (Conseiller pédagogique)**  
University staff member who helps students plan their program, choose courses, and navigate academic requirements.

**Association étudiante (Student association)**  
Organization representing students in a program or faculty. Organizes social activities, defends student rights, often sells past exams.

---

## B

**Bachelor's degree (Baccalauréat / Bac)**  
First-cycle university degree. 3 years / 90 credits. Note: the French word "Baccalauréat" in Quebec refers to a university bachelor's degree, NOT a high school diploma.

---

## C

**CEGEP**  
Pre-university college (2 years) or vocational college (3 years). Mandatory step for students from Quebec before university. Students from other countries enter university directly.

**Code permanent**  
Permanent alphanumeric ID issued by Quebec's Ministry of Education (MEQ). Example: TREM12345678. This code follows you throughout your Quebec academic career.

**Contingentement (Competitive admission)**  
Programs with a limited number of spots and selective admission (e.g., medicine, law, engineering). Non-contingenté = open/non-selective admission.

---

## D

**Decanship (Décanat)**  
The dean's office for a faculty. Handles academic affairs at the faculty level.

---

## E

**Examen intra (Midterm exam)**  
Mid-semester exam, usually in October (fall) or February (winter).

---

## G

**Grille de cours / Program grid**  
Official document listing all mandatory and elective courses required to graduate. Use this to plan your academic path.

---

## H

**Heures de bureau (Office hours)**  
Scheduled times when a professor is available to meet students without an appointment (or by appointment). Listed in the course outline.

---

## L

**Lab (Laboratoire)**  
Practical session complementing the lecture component of a course (science, computer science, languages).

---

## M

**Matricule (Student ID number)**  
Your unique university identification number (different from the code permanent).

---

## P

**PEB (Prêt entre bibliothèques / Interlibrary Loan)**  
Service allowing you to borrow documents from another university library.

**Plan de cours (Course outline / Syllabus)**  
Official document describing a course: objectives, evaluation, readings, rules. Distributed on the first day of class.

**Préalable (Prerequisite)**  
A course that must be completed before registering for a more advanced course.

---

## R

**Registrariat (Registrar's office)**  
Administrative office managing course registration, transcripts, graduation, and program changes.

**Relevé de notes (Transcript)**  
Official document listing all your courses and grades.

---

## S

**Session (Semester)**  
An academic term (fall, winter, summer). Example: "I have class on Monday this session."

**Stage (Internship / Co-op)**  
Work placement integrated into a program. Can be mandatory or optional depending on the program.

---

## T

**TA (Teaching Assistant / Auxiliaire d'enseignement – AE)**  
Graduate student who assists with tutorials, labs, and marking.

**Travail (Assignment)**  
A piece of work submitted by a deadline. Different from an exam.

---

## U

**UER**  
Unit of teaching and research – term sometimes used at UQAR for a department.
